Company History

Barista Cafe was founded in 2009 by coffee enthusiast Jan Kowalski and his wife, pastry chef Anna Kowalska. What began as a small coffee cart in Poznań's bustling business district has evolved into a beloved chain with over 15 locations across Poland.

Our journey began with a simple passion: to create the perfect cup of coffee using ethically sourced beans and traditional brewing methods. Over the past decade, we have expanded our offerings while staying true to our original mission of quality, sustainability, and community.

In 2015, we opened our flagship store in downtown Poznań, featuring our first in-house roastery. By 2018, we had expanded to five locations, and in 2020, despite global challenges, we launched our successful premium coffee subscription service.

Company Structure

Barista Cafe operates under a vertically integrated business model, overseeing everything from bean sourcing to customer service:

  • Roastery Operations
  • Cafe Operations & Management

  • Training & Education Department
  • Marketing & Customer Experience

Our headquarters in Poznań houses our main administrative offices, training center, and flagship roastery.

Coffee Questions

We source our coffee beans from ethical farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, Brazil, and Indonesia. We maintain direct relationships with many of our farmers, ensuring fair prices and sustainable practices. All of our beans are specialty grade and roasted in small batches at our Poznań roastery to ensure maximum freshness and flavor.

Our coffee stands out for several reasons: we select only the top 1% of coffee beans available, we roast in small batches to ensure freshness, our baristas undergo extensive training (minimum 6 months), and we use precision brewing methods tailored to each specific coffee origin. We also focus on highlighting the natural flavors in each coffee rather than masking them with excessive additives.

Yes! We offer Swiss Water Process decaffeinated coffee, which uses water rather than chemicals to remove caffeine while preserving the coffee's natural flavors. We typically have at least one decaf option for espresso drinks and one for filter coffee available daily.

Orders & Reservations

You can make a reservation through our website by visiting the Reservation page, by calling us at +48 612 223 344, or through our mobile app. Reservations can be made up to 14 days in advance and we recommend booking at least 24 hours ahead for weekend visits.

Yes! We deliver coffee beans throughout Poznań and surrounding areas. We also offer nationwide shipping for our subscription service. Local orders placed before noon are typically delivered the same day, while orders outside Poznań are shipped within 24 hours and usually arrive within 2-3 business days.
